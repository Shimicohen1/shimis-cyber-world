## Who is Shimi Cohen?

Shimi Cohen is a cybersecurity professional and the founder of Shimi's Cyber World.

With deep hands-on experience across the cybersecurity landscape, Shimi built SCW out of a genuine need — a place where real practitioners can connect, share knowledge, and stay ahead of the threat landscape without the noise and marketing that floods the industry.

What started as a passion for helping others in the cyber community grew into one of the largest independent cybersecurity ecosystems — connecting professionals, companies, and startups through real content, real tools, and real discussions.

Shimi believes that **the best security knowledge should be open, accessible, and community-driven** — not buried behind paywalls or diluted by marketing. That belief is the foundation of everything SCW does.

## What is SCW?

Shimi's Cyber World is a cyber ecosystem built around a strong community.

What started as a large cyber community has grown into a place where people, companies, and startups connect, share, and stay ahead.

Here you'll find:
- **Real updates** — no recycled news, no filler
- **Real discussions** — from practitioners in the field
- **Tools and practical resources** — battle-tested, not theoretical
- **Insights from the field** — things you won't find in official channels

Everything is driven by the people inside it.

## The Mission

Creative. Equal. Connected. **Real cyber, built by real people.**

The best security knowledge shouldn't be buried in PDFs or locked behind paywalls. It should be alive, accessible, and shaped by the community that uses it. We believe everyone has something to contribute — no matter where they come from or where they're headed.

SCW exists to:
- **Connect** people, companies, and startups in cybersecurity
- **Share** real updates and insights from the field
- **Build** tools and resources that solve real problems
- **Grow** an ecosystem where everyone stays ahead

## What's Next

Built from the community. Growing into much more.

- Expanding the SCW ecosystem
- More tools in the Premium Toolkit
- Deeper community features and signal sharing
- Curated learning paths and vault resources
