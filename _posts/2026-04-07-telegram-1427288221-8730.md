Even on significant days like OpIsrael, the cybersecurity landscape often reveals that major breaches don't always stem from a single, massive attack. Instead, 'חדשות סייבר - ארז דסה' points out that the genesis of many serious cyber events lies in overlooked, seemingly minor security oversights. These can include an access point left unintentionally open, a critical alert that goes unexamined, or log data that isn't correlated to form a complete picture of potential threats.

According to 'חדשות סייבר - ארז דסה', these small cracks are precisely how real-world incidents begin to take shape. The source emphasizes that a proactive Security Operations Center (SOC) doesn't wait for damage to occur. Instead, it focuses on identifying and mitigating these initial vulnerabilities before they can be exploited by adversaries. This approach is crucial for maintaining a robust security posture, especially in environments facing heightened geopolitical cyber activity.
