OSINT Industries has dropped a new Capture The Flag (CTF) challenge, pushing participants to hone their geolocation skills. The latest puzzle presents a snapshot taken somewhere in England, featuring two individuals. The mission for aspiring OSINT pros is to pinpoint the exact GPS coordinates of the location shown.

This type of challenge is a classic OSINT exercise, demanding keen observation and the ability to leverage visual cues within an image. Participants will need to analyze details like architecture, signage, landscape, and potentially even subtle clues from the subjects themselves to triangulate the precise spot. Success hinges on meticulous research and understanding how to effectively use mapping tools and local knowledge databases.

The ultimate goal is to submit the coordinates in the format OSINT{latitude_N_longitude_W}. This CTF is a prime opportunity for those looking to sharpen their digital sleuthing abilities in a practical, engaging way. You can dive into the challenge at their dedicated CTF platform.
