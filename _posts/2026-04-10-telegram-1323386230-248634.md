A recent incident at Hims & Hers Health, Inc. has put a significant spotlight on the ever-present risks to protected health information (PHI). According to Cyber Threat Intelligence, the breach exposed some of the most sensitive types of PHI, a stark reminder that cybercriminals are constantly hunting for high-value data.

While the full scope and technical details are still emerging, any compromise involving PHI is a big deal. This isn't just about names and addresses; it's often about medical conditions, treatments, and other deeply personal data that can be weaponized for various nefarious purposes, from targeted phishing to identity theft. For a healthcare-adjacent company like Hims, which deals with sensitive personal health matters, such a breach can erode trust faster than a zero-day exploit.
