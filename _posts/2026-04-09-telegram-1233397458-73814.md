Pentesting News is highlighting key trends emerging from Mobile World Congress 2026, with a particular focus on how Artificial Intelligence is reshaping network security operations. The discussions and announcements point towards a significant shift in how organizations will defend their networks, moving beyond traditional, often reactive, security measures.

According to Pentesting News, the integration of AI into Security Operations Centers (SOCs) is no longer a futuristic concept but a present reality driving innovation. This evolution aims to empower security teams with advanced capabilities to detect, analyze, and respond to threats with unprecedented speed and accuracy. The emphasis is on AI's role in automating complex tasks, identifying subtle anomalies that human analysts might miss, and ultimately bolstering the overall security posture against an increasingly sophisticated threat landscape.
