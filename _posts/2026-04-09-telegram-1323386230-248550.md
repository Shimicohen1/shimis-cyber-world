Cyberabad Police have apprehended 10 individuals across multiple states in connection with seven distinct cyber fraud incidents. The operation resulted in the recovery of ₹74.28 lakh, which has been returned to the victims. This action highlights the ongoing, multi-jurisdictional nature of cybercrime and the efforts by law enforcement to combat it.

According to reports, the arrests stem from investigations into various online scams. While specific details on the modus operandi for each of the seven cases weren't fully elaborated, the recovery of a significant sum underscores the financial motivation behind these criminal activities. Authorities are using this bust as a springboard to reiterate warnings to the public about the persistent threats posed by online scams and the importance of vigilance.
