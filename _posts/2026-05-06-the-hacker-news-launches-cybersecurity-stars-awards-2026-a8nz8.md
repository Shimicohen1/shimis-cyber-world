The Hacker News has announced the launch of its 'Cybersecurity Stars Awards 2026', aiming to recognize significant contributions within the cybersecurity industry. For nearly two decades, The Hacker News has focused on reporting major cyber incidents, system compromises, and emerging threats. This new initiative shifts focus to highlight the positive impact of security leaders, innovative defense teams, and effective security products.

This move by The Hacker News acknowledges the often-unseen work that underpins enterprise security. It’s a deliberate effort to spotlight the individuals and technologies that actively protect organizations, rather than just chronicling the failures and attacks. The awards seek to celebrate those making tough strategic decisions, building resilient defenses, and developing robust tools that operate continuously against evolving threats.
