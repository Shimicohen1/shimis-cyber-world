Cyber Threat Intelligence has surfaced details concerning a significant data leak originating from the NSFW application MyLovely.AI. The breach reportedly exposed the personal data, explicit user prompts, and associated images of over 100,000 individuals. This compromised information could potentially leave affected users vulnerable to severe privacy violations, including sextortion and doxxing.

According to Cyber Threat Intelligence, the leak specifically involved around 70,000 prompts that have been linked back to individual users. The nature of the content, combined with personally identifiable information, presents a high-risk scenario for those whose data was accessed. The implications of such a leak extend beyond mere embarrassment, potentially leading to targeted harassment and financial extortion.
