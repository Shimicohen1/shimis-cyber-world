According to information circulating from International Cyber Digest, the threat actor group TeamPCP has reportedly targeted Illusive Networks, an Israeli cybersecurity company.

Illusive Networks, known for its work in deception technology and honeypots, was acquired by Proofpoint in 2022. At present, there has been no independent confirmation or official statement regarding this specific attack beyond the claims made by International Cyber Digest. The outlet states its intelligence originates from sources embedded within the TeamPCP group.
