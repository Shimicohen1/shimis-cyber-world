Cyber News - Erez Dasa reports that an impressive 15 out of the 30 companies selected for the prestigious 'Rising In Cyber 2026' project are Israeli. This initiative, spearheaded by global venture capital firm Notable Capital, involves 150 CISOs and senior cyber leaders identifying top cybersecurity startups from a pool of 275. The disproportionate representation of Israeli firms highlights the nation's significant innovation and impact within the global cybersecurity landscape.

This strong showing, with Israeli companies making up 50% of the chosen cohort, surpasses previous years where 11 Israeli firms were recognized in both 2024 and 2025. The companies named include Cyera, Grip Security, Island, Noma Security, Oligo Security, Orca Security, Token Security, Torq, Beacon Security, Clover Security, Descope, Echo Security, Terra Security, and Tonic AI. This success underscores the ongoing strength and global competitiveness of Israel's cybersecurity sector.
