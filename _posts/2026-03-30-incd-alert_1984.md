The Israel National Cyber Directorate (INCD) has issued a warning regarding an anticipated surge in cyberattacks targeting the Israeli internet space. This annual threat typically escalates around significant national remembrance days and holidays, including OPIsrael (starting April 7, 2026), spring festivals, Holocaust Remembrance Day, Memorial Day, and Independence Day. Hacktivist groups, notably those associated with Anonymous, alongside pro-Palestinian actors, are expected to intensify their efforts to cause reputational and publicity damage to Israel.

This year's heightened threat landscape is compounded by ongoing military operations, including "Operation Iron Swords" against Iran and the conflict with Hezbollah in northern Israel. The INCD emphasizes that these concurrent events could amplify the scale and impact of the anticipated cyber campaigns. Organizations are urged to heighten their vigilance and implement robust security measures to mitigate potential disruptions and data breaches.
