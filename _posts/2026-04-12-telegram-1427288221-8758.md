A recent attack attributed to the Iranian group Ababil of Minab has targeted Vyncs, a US-based company specializing in GPS tracking systems for vehicles. According to חדשות סייבר - ארז דסה, the group claimed responsibility for the cyberattack, which impacted Vyncs' operations.

Vyncs acknowledged the cyber incident on its Facebook page, initially confirming the disruption. In a subsequent update, the company stated it had successfully restored most of its systems, indicating a degree of recovery following the digital assault. This incident highlights the ongoing trend of Iranian-linked groups targeting US infrastructure, particularly entities with critical data or operational roles.
