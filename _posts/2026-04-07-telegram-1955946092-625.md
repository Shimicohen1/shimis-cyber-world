The OSINT Industries Telegram channel has dropped a new Capture The Flag (CTF) challenge designed to test your geolocation and temporal analysis chops. Dubbed 'Pinpoint the Time,' this challenge presents participants with a single photograph featuring a train station. The objective is twofold: accurately identify the specific station depicted and then narrow down the time the photo was taken to within a two-hour window.

This isn't your typical CTF fare; it emphasizes the power of meticulous observation and the subtle clues often embedded within visual media. Success hinges on spotting minute details that can unlock the location and piecing together temporal indicators, turning a static image into a dynamic data point. OSINT Industries is known for crafting challenges that push the boundaries of open-source intelligence gathering, and this one appears to be no exception.

Participants are encouraged to dive deep into the visual data, leveraging their knowledge of infrastructure, signage, and even environmental cues to solve the puzzle. The challenge is accessible via their dedicated CTF platform, inviting the cybersecurity community to put their OSINT skills to the test.
