The Israel National Cyber Directorate (INCD) has issued a warning regarding the active exploitation of a critical vulnerability affecting specific Rockwell Automation controller series. This flaw, originally identified in 2021, is now being leveraged by threat actors worldwide to compromise industrial control systems.

Rockwell Automation has not released a security update for this specific vulnerability. Their current recommendation is to operate the affected controllers in 'Run Mode' to prevent unauthorized configuration changes. The INCD strongly advises limiting access to these controllers, ensuring only necessary business-related IP addresses can connect. Furthermore, it is crucial to verify that direct internet access to these controllers is strictly prohibited.
