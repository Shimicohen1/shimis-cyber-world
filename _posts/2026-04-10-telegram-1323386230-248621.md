Orange Business is making a significant play in the enterprise voice communications space, rolling out a revamped suite of services designed to integrate trust and artificial intelligence. The company aims to modernize how businesses handle voice interactions, emphasizing enhanced security and AI-driven capabilities. This strategic shift targets the evolving demands of the modern enterprise, where secure and intelligent communication is no longer a luxury but a necessity.

According to reports, the new offerings are built around a core of trust, aiming to provide a secure foundation for these critical communication channels. The integration of AI is pitched to unlock new efficiencies and insights from voice data, while simultaneously bolstering the security posture against emerging threats. This move signals a broader trend in the UCaaS (Unified Communications as a Service) market, where vendors are increasingly layering advanced security features and AI onto their platforms to attract and retain enterprise clients wary of data breaches and sophisticated cyber-attacks.
