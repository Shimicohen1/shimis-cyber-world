Cyber Detective recently spotlighted Stalkie, an open-source intelligence (OSINT) tool designed to help cybersecurity professionals and researchers locate social media accounts associated with a given username. Developed by Ashen Dilantha, Stalkie streamlines the often-tedious process of sifting through various platforms.

What sets Stalkie apart, as reported by Cyber Detective, is its ability to assign a confidence score to potential matches. This rating system isn't just a random guess; it's based on parameters like the frequency with which the target username appears on a particular page, among other indicators. This functionality helps analysts cut through the noise and prioritize leads, making OSINT investigations more efficient and reliable. It's a handy addition to any red teamer's or threat intel analyst's toolkit when they're trying to map out an adversary's online presence or conduct reconnaissance.
