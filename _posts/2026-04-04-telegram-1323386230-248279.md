In a significant move to combat online financial crime, the Jammu Cyber Police has successfully resolved over 1,200 fraud complaints during the first quarter of 2026. This proactive stance not only demonstrates the growing efficacy of law enforcement in tackling cyber threats but also highlights the increasing prevalence of such sophisticated scams targeting individuals and businesses.

The department's diligent efforts have resulted in the recovery of approximately ₹3.52 crore (35.2 million INR), preventing substantial financial losses for victims. This achievement underscores the critical role of specialized cyber units in safeguarding citizens from digital malfeasance and reinforces the importance of robust reporting mechanisms for cybercrimes.
