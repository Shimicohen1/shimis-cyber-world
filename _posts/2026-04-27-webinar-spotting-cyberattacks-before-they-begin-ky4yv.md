BleepingComputer is hosting a live webinar focused on empowering security teams to identify the precursor signals of cyberattacks. Scheduled for April 30th at 2:00 PM ET, the session features threat intelligence firm Flare and researcher Tammy Harper. The core objective is to equip professionals with the knowledge to spot potential threats before they mature into full-blown incidents.

This discussion is critical for defenders looking to shift from reactive incident response to a more proactive stance. Understanding the subtle indicators that precede an attack can significantly reduce an organization's exposure and the potential impact of a breach. Attendees will gain insights into actionable intelligence gathering and analysis techniques.
