OSINT Industries recently wrapped up its second annual OSINT Connect event in France, bringing together over 300 law enforcement professionals. The summit focused on practical applications and shared experiences in open-source intelligence (OSINT) and digital investigations. Discussions spanned critical areas such as RAM analysis, tackling ICAC investigations, understanding infostealer tactics, digital forensics, and navigating legal frameworks. Workshops were designed around real-world investigative scenarios, providing hands-on learning for attendees.

The program's comprehensive nature highlights the evolving challenges faced by digital investigators. Covering topics from sophisticated malware like infostealers to the intricacies of digital forensics and the crucial legal aspects of evidence gathering, the event aimed to equip law enforcement with the latest techniques and knowledge. OSINT Industries specifically thanked Megadose Palenath for spearheading the initiative, underscoring the collaborative effort involved in advancing these critical skill sets within the law enforcement community.
