Cyber News - Erez Dasa highlights common, yet critical, security oversights found across many organizations. Their analysis points to three fundamental questions every security team should answer: Are there inactive user accounts older than 60 days? Is Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) enforced universally for all critical access points? Can anomalous logins be detected in real-time, not just in hindsight? Failing to confidently answer 'yes' to these indicates significant, exploitable weaknesses.

This isn't about complex zero-days; it's about foundational security hygiene. The reality, as observed by Cyber News - Erez Dasa, is that most organizations have gaps. The crucial factor isn't the existence of these gaps, but the ability to identify and remediate them proactively before they are exploited by attackers.
