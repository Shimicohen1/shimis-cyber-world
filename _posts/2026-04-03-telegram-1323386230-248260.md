Authorities in Rajasthan have successfully dismantled a sophisticated cyber fraud operation centered around a fake escort service scam. The bust led to the apprehension of 12 individuals who were allegedly involved in extorting money from victims under false pretenses.

This incident highlights a common tactic within the cybercrime landscape where fraudulent services are advertised online to lure unsuspecting individuals. Once contact is made, perpetrators often employ blackmail or other coercive methods to extract financial gain. The prompt action by the Rajasthan police demonstrates a growing effort to combat these digital deceptions and protect citizens from online exploitation. Further investigations are expected to reveal the full scope of the network and its methods.
