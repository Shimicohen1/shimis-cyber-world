The rapid proliferation of AI agents is creating a significant blind spot for organizations, with a surge in unsecured APIs becoming a prime attack vector. Cyber Threat Intelligence reports that the speed at which AI tools are being deployed is outpacing traditional security measures, leaving a trail of vulnerable interfaces.

These AI agents, often integrated without rigorous security vetting, can expose sensitive data and systems through improperly configured or unmonitored APIs. The sheer volume and complexity of these integrations mean that many organizations are simply not equipped to identify and remediate the risks before they're exploited. This trend highlights a critical gap between the pace of innovation and the evolution of robust API security practices.
