Winona County is grappling with a significant cyberattack that has crippled critical services and necessitated emergency intervention. According to Pentesting News, the incident forced Minnesota Governor Tim Walz to deploy the National Guard to assist in restoring operations. The full extent of the disruption and the specific systems affected are still being assessed, but the severity has clearly prompted a high-level response.

Pentesting News highlighted that the cyberattack's impact has been substantial enough to warrant state-level emergency support. This situation underscores the vulnerability of local government infrastructure to sophisticated cyber threats and the cascading effects such incidents can have on public services. While details on the attack vector or the perpetrators remain scarce, the deployment of the National Guard signals a critical breach requiring significant resources to mitigate and recover from.
