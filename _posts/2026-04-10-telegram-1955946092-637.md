Chief Inspector Tijl De Groot of the Brussels Capital Police recently shared insights into actual Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) investigations during a session at OSINT Connect. This exclusive conference, aimed at law enforcement professionals, saw De Groot, who heads the Judiciary/Cybercrime division, break down complex cases for an audience of 300 attendees.

The session focused on the practicalities and challenges of investigating CSAM, a critical and disturbing area of cybercrime. By presenting real-world scenarios, the aim was to equip fellow law enforcement officers with better understanding and strategies for tackling these investigations. This kind of direct knowledge sharing is invaluable for those on the front lines of combating online exploitation.
