Cyber News - Erez Dasa has clarified two recent cybersecurity incidents. The ransomware group Stormous incorrectly identified German company Or-technology as Israeli, causing unnecessary alarm. This misclassification has reportedly been corrected. Additionally, claims of a data theft from Crocs Israel are being met with skepticism. Dasa cautiously assesses this as potential fake news, noting the alleged stolen data does not appear to belong to Crocs, contains fields the company likely doesn't collect, and the threat actor has a negligible reputation with only a handful of email addresses in the purported database.
