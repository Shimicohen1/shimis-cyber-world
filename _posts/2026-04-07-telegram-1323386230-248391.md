The upcoming RSAC 2026 is set to highlight a crucial, often overlooked aspect of our industry: its people. Cyber Threat Intelligence points out that discussions are increasingly shifting towards the human element in cybersecurity, recognizing that technology alone isn't enough to win the fight against sophisticated threats.

This focus acknowledges that while advanced tools and AI are vital, the effectiveness of any security program ultimately hinges on the skills, awareness, and dedication of the individuals managing and interacting with it. Cyber Threat Intelligence suggests that conferences like RSAC are becoming platforms to explore how to better recruit, train, and retain top cybersecurity talent, as well as foster a culture of security consciousness throughout organizations. It's a recognition that the 'human firewall' is as critical as any digital defense.
