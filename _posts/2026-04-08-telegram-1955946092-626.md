OSINT Industries has published a detailed guide exploring the profound OSINT potential hidden within Indian (+91) phone numbers. Their analysis reveals how a single number can serve as a gateway to an individual's identity, associated businesses, and even linked online accounts. This isn't just about finding a name; it's about mapping digital footprints.

The workflow outlined by OSINT Industries is methodical, starting with targeted "dorking" techniques to unearth publicly exposed information. From there, the process expands to leverage messaging app data, public business records, and crucially, data exposed in various breaches. This multi-pronged approach underscores the interconnectedness of personal and professional information in the digital realm.

OSINT Industries also touches on the role of automation in streamlining this process, allowing for more efficient collection and correlation of data points. This guide offers a stark reminder of the extensive information that can be pieced together, even from seemingly innocuous data points like a phone number, when viewed through the lens of skilled OSINT practitioners.
