Cybersecurity researchers have identified APILEECH, a novel Chrome browser extension designed for in-depth analysis of web pages. According to Cyber Detective, this tool facilitates the retrieval of POST and GET requests in real-time, aids in the examination of website comments and error messages, and can extract data from various social media profiles, including Twitter, Facebook, and TikTok.

The extension, developed by 'cali', offers a suite of features aimed at providing a comprehensive view of a webpage's underlying structure and data flow. Its capabilities extend to analyzing source code and capturing publicly available information from popular social platforms, presenting a potentially valuable asset for threat intelligence gathering and digital forensics.
