🚨 CVE-2026-0558 A vulnerability in parisneo/lollms, up to and including version 2.2.0, allows unauthenticated users to upload and process files through the /api/files/extract-text endpoint. This endpoint does not enforce authentication, unlike other file-related endpoints, and lacks the Depends(getcurrentactiveuser) dependency.

## Why It Matters

- New vulnerability disclosed — verify if your stack is exposed.
- Supply chain risk — audit dependencies and third-party integrations.
