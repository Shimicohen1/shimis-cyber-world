A recent incident involving the Axios news organization highlights a disturbing trend: the industrialization of social engineering attacks. Cyber Threat Intelligence points to this attack as a prime example of how sophisticated threat actors are leveraging complex tactics to breach high-value targets.

The attackers didn't just send a few dodgy emails; they employed a multi-stage approach, meticulously crafting their attack to bypass security measures and exploit human trust. This wasn't a smash-and-grab; it was a calculated operation designed for maximum impact, demonstrating a significant leap in the sophistication and resourcefulness of these cybercriminals. Cyber Threat Intelligence notes that such advanced social engineering campaigns are becoming less about opportunistic phishing and more about targeted, high-stakes infiltration.
