🚨 CVE-2026-34405 Nuxt OG Image generates OG Images with Vue templates in Nuxt. Prior to version 6.2.5, the image‑generation component by the URI: /og/d/ (and, in older versions, /og-image/) contains a vulnerability that allows injection of arbitrary attributes into the HTML page body. This issue has been patched in version 6.2.5. 🎖@cveNotify Links: - ---
