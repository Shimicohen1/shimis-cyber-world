Law enforcement has taken down the W3LL phishing network, a major player in online fraud. The FBI, working alongside Indonesian police, conducted simultaneous operations that reportedly dismantled the cybercrime outfit responsible for an estimated $20 million in attempted fraud.

Cyber Threat Intelligence reports that this takedown is a significant blow to phishing operations targeting financial institutions and online services. The W3LL network was known for its sophisticated phishing kits and ability to mimic legitimate websites, tricking users into divulging sensitive information like login credentials and financial details.
