Rockstar Games has been impacted by a data breach stemming from a security incident at analytics provider Anodot. The ShinyHunters extortion gang has reportedly begun leaking data stolen during this breach on their dark web leak site. BleepingComputer reports that the leaked information consists of analytics data connected to Rockstar Games.

This incident highlights the risks associated with supply chain attacks, where a compromise at a vendor can lead to a breach at their clients. While the exact nature and extent of the stolen data are still emerging, the involvement of an extortion gang suggests potential future attempts at further exploitation or demands.

Further details on the specific data compromised and the methods used by ShinyHunters are expected to surface as the situation develops. Organizations relying on third-party services should be particularly vigilant about their vendor's security posture.
