Cyber Threat Intelligence reports that detectives in Igembe North have apprehended a suspect, Morris Kithinji Tirimiti, in connection with cyber fraud activities. The bust yielded a significant cache of digital tools, including six mobile phones and a staggering 39 SIM cards. Authorities suspect these items are instrumental to ongoing digital scam operations.

This recovery highlights a common tactic in cyber fraud: the mass acquisition and use of multiple SIM cards. Threat actors often employ these to create numerous fake accounts, bypass verification processes, and obscure their true identities, making it harder for law enforcement to trace their illicit activities. The sheer volume of SIM cards recovered suggests a well-organized operation rather than a lone wolf.
