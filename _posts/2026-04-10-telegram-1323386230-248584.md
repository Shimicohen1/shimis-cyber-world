The U.S. Department of the Treasury is set to enhance its cybersecurity posture by directly sharing threat intelligence with cryptocurrency firms. This initiative aims to bolster the digital defenses of the virtual asset sector, which has increasingly become a target for malicious actors.

According to reports, this direct channel will facilitate a more streamlined flow of information regarding emerging cyber threats, attack vectors, and vulnerabilities relevant to crypto businesses. The move is a significant step towards a more proactive and collaborative approach to cybersecurity within a rapidly evolving financial landscape. By enabling timely intelligence sharing, the Treasury hopes to equip these firms with the knowledge needed to anticipate and mitigate potential attacks, thereby safeguarding both the industry and its users.
