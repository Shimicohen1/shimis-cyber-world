A fresh espionage campaign is reportedly underway, specifically targeting Ukrainian emergency services and local government bodies. According to The Record by Recorded Future, the attackers are deploying a previously undocumented malware strain dubbed **AgingFly**.

This isn't just opportunistic scanning; it's a focused effort to compromise critical infrastructure. The targeting of hospitals and emergency services is particularly concerning, as it directly impacts public safety and national resilience during ongoing conflict. The introduction of AgingFly indicates a dedicated and evolving threat landscape facing Ukraine's digital defenses.
