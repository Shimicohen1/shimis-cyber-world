Following a global Outlook.com outage on Monday, Microsoft has mandated that iPhone users re-enter their credentials to access Outlook and Hotmail accounts through the native iOS Mail application. This action, reported by BleepingComputer, aims to secure accounts after the service disruption.

While Microsoft has not detailed the specific cause or security implications that necessitated this re-authentication, the directive suggests a potential compromise or a precautionary measure to ensure account integrity. Users are advised to update their credentials promptly to restore access and protect their data.
