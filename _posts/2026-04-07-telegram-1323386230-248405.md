A significant disruption has impacted critical services across Russia, as reported by Cyber Threat Intelligence. The outage affected major banking applications and metro payment systems nationwide, leading to widespread disruptions. Users experienced hours of inaccessibility, with issues reported for card transactions, cash withdrawals, and mobile banking services.

While the exact cause remains under investigation, the broad scope of the failure points to a potentially systemic issue or a coordinated event. The implications are substantial, highlighting the vulnerability of essential infrastructure to service disruptions, whether accidental or malicious. This incident serves as a stark reminder of the interconnectedness of financial and public transit systems and their susceptibility to widespread outages.
