A new free online resource, the OSINTNova Google Dorks Generator, has been highlighted by the Telegram channel Cyber Detective. This tool is designed to assist users in crafting advanced Google search queries, commonly known as "dorks." These specialized queries enable more precise information gathering concerning specific targets such as email addresses, phone numbers, domains, or company details.

The generator is presented as a component of the broader OSINTNova online intelligence toolkit, accessible via their website. The availability of such tools can significantly streamline the open-source intelligence (OSINT) gathering process for security professionals, researchers, and investigators seeking to uncover publicly available information efficiently.
