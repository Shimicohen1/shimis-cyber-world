OSINT Industries recently hosted a webinar delving into the intricacies of investigating Iranian cyber actors, a subject of growing concern in the threat landscape. The session covered critical aspects ranging from identifying networks linked to the Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps (IRGC) and their associated proxy companies, to employing effective OSINT pivots for attribution. The discussion emphasized the importance of ethical tradecraft in navigating these complex investigations.

The webinar, according to OSINT Industries, provided viewers with practical insights into the methodologies used to track and understand the operations of these state-sponsored or state-aligned groups. This kind of intelligence is crucial for defenders looking to bolster their defenses against sophisticated adversaries.
