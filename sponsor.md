<section class="page-section reveal prose">
  <h2>What we will not do</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Sponsored content disguised as editorial — every paid placement is clearly labeled.</li>
    <li>FUD, scareware, or aggressive copy — we protect reader trust above all.</li>
    <li>Pop-ups, autoplay video, or intrusive interstitials.</li>
    <li>Selling, sharing, or renting reader email lists.</li>
    <li>Endorsing tools we have not vetted.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Editorial independence</h2>
  <p>Sponsorships do not influence our editorial coverage. We will write critically about a sponsor's product if and when warranted — that's the deal we have with our readers.</p>
